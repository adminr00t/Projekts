Logo

Report

The design decisions made to craft this website was to keep it simple, but allow it to conform to the modern aesthetic of websites, so it doesn't look dated and looks maintained, its a good design as it allows for simplistic navigation of the website without being too crowded or overloaded with information and elements. The site uses XHTML5 and semantics to allow for the building and maintaining of multiple elements within the document, and uses a single style sheet to apply colors, font, font styles and positioning to said elements. This also allows us to conform to the diverse enviroment of devices capable of displaying web pages by having a responsive design in mind.



The site uses structural HTML with styling is a sperate CSS file, with minimal page elements and simple design. The colors are choses to reflect the most abundant color in the background, making it easier to read and not jarring to see and uses simple navigatio tabs with a "back to..." at the bottom of deeper pages (such as the page dedicated to an author).



Based on device width, the page conforms to make it easier for navigation. The nav bar on a laptop/desktop screen is along the left side, while on mobile devices, the bar is at the top of the page, allowing for much less crowded screen real estate and a more natural feeling.



With our simple yet modern design, site viewers will feel as if they're viewing any other maintained site, while being able to read the site on any device
