About Us

Established in 1999 and located in Surrey, Canada, helping new writers succeed has been a core value from the very beginning. The business began with the want to help aspiring new authors in the process of getting their books out in the open air and it has persisted as the core value to the very day. We do this because it helps people find their footing, helping them continue to follow their passion for writing, and seeing that process and the success at the end of the road is what propels us forward
