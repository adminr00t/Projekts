Eric Nylund

Biography

  • Born on November 12th, 1964, Nylund was first hired by Microsoft to re-write and edit their Multi-media encyclopedia and started discussing the idea of a Halo related novel before Halo: Combat Evovled was developed. With the book delayed, it gave Nylund a chance to see the games development at every stage, and in partnership with Bungie, the original developers, wrote a book giving Master Chief and the Halo universe a definitive background.



Works

  1. Halo: The Fall of Reach - Acting as a prequel to the first Halo game, this novel details the events the led upto where the first game starts and explains the origins of the Spartan-IIs.

  2. Halo: First Strike- Opening in the middle of the Battle of Reach, this book explores the efforts humanity held up to defend the planet and the adventures of the survivors of the Battle of Alpha Halo.

  3. Halo: Ghosts of Onyx - This book details the creation of supersoldiers known as Spartan-IIIs and takes place after the events of Halo 2. The newly created spartans help defend the military training world of Oynx from the Covenant reaching ancient forerunner secrets that could mean losing the war.

  4. Halo: Evolutions - This book contains severel stories that further expand the Halo Universe, written from the perspective of those defending humanity, and those vowed to wipe humanity out of existance.
