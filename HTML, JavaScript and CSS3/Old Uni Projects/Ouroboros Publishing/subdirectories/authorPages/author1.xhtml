James Dashner

Biography

  • Born 26 November 1972, James is an american writer best known for the Maze Runner series. His other works include the Mortality Doctirne and the 13th Reality Series. Dashner has said that the inspiration that propelled him to create the Maze Runner series was The Shining and his various experiences throughout life.



Works

  1. Maze Runner - a group of boys and one girl must find a way to overcome group conflict, fight off giant mechanical creatures that hunt in the dark and escape a giant concrete labyrinthine before their resources run out.

  2. Scorch Trials - after escaping the maze, the group finds themselves in a barren wasteland which was once the modern world, chased by zombie like creatures, the group must move towards the "Safe Haven" those who put them in the maze told them about.

  3. Death Cure - After both the Maze and the Scorch, the group is now split, with the corporation WICKED, harvesting a cure from the blood of those who have survived. Thomas must find a way to unite the group and take down WICKED once and for all

  4. The Eye of Minds - Mchael, a gamer, is recruited by the government to hunt down a hacker in the VirtNet- a total mind and body immersion into virtual reality.
